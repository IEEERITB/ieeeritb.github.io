Quora World Meetup at Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Benagaluru

QuoRa is the abbrevation of Quora Ramaiah, which is the group of all the Quorans pursuing their passion for Engineering at Ramaiah Institute of Technology (RIT). QuoRa is a closely knit circle where the Quorans at RIT meet weekly, interact with one another and share their knowledge.

We are happy to announce QuoRa Meetup- the Quora World Meetup 2017 at Ramaiah Institute of Technology which will be organized on Friday, November 10th, 2017. Our event is officially recognized by Quora. The link to the The QuoRa meetup will be tentatively a 3 hours affair starting at 3:00 PM(GST+5:30). The various topics of discussion will be published soon. We invite all the Quorans across Benagaluru and globe to attend the QuoRa meetup and be part of one of the very first meetups at RIT.
