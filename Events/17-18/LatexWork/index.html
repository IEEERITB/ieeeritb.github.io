Technical Writing using LaTeX

LaTeX.. Intrigued? Then, come grow with us.

IEEE RIT is coming up with a seminar along with providing practical knowledge on LaTeX which is scheduled on 14th of October. LaTeX is a computer programming language used for typesetting technical documents. It includes features designed for the production and is the de facto standard for the communication and publication of scientific documents.

We have Mr Aditya Kalyani (RIT Alumni, Batch of 2017, IEEE RIT Chair 2016-17) as the speaker who will share his valuable knowledge with us.
